There is a £25 joining fee on all memberships which includes an admin fee, welcome pack, goal setting, induction and on-going reviews.

Your membership not only includes gym usage but casual hire of the sports hall free of charge. Bookable up to 7 days in advance, 1 court per booking, any non-member guests pay £2 per hour.

Family Badminton: A maximum of 3 under 16's per booking can play badminton for free! Limited to one court per booking. Must be supervised by a member over 16 years.

In-house fitness classes also included in your membership! bookable up to 7 days in advance. See the fitness timetable for our full schedule

As a Sports Centre member Squash is now included in your membership.

Promotions

  • As a member you can bring a guest with you to the gym for only £5 per session

Conditions:

  1. Minimum age permitted to hold a membership is 16. Under 16's are not permitted to use the fitness studio. Use of any other facility by under 16's must be supervised by a responsible adult (16+) at all times.
  2. Members are required to sign a copy of the membership Terms & Conditions upon joining.
  3. Couples must have a bank account in joint names from which the Direct Debit payments will be taken, or reside at the same address in order to be classed as a couple.
  4. Proof of age / NUS card may be requested upon application if applying as a student.
