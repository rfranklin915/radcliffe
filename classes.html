Class Descriptions

In-house classes free to members. Only £2.50 for non-members

Spin

Burn some calories & improve fitness in one go! Work at your own intensity while cycling to motivating music.

Spin & abs

30 minute spin class as above with a 15 minute core blast to finish off

Boxercise

Energetic class using all training systems involving punches and footwork to incorporate a motivating total body workout.

P.H.A.T blaster

Peripheral Heart Action Training, working upper body, lower body + core to create a whole body workout in a fun environment.

Pump fx

Progressive resistance training using a bar and weight plates to deliver functional, multi-plane movement and total body results.

Core Blast

Core strengthening exercises. A great start to the day!

Taff’s Fit

High Intensity, guaranteed fat loss, cross fit style training...are you tough enough?!

LUCKY DIP

Depending which weekend it falls on, this class will be any of the above in blue.

Plyometrics

A high intensity session to improve sporting performance and burn off excessive body fat, predominantly using explosive movements.

Outdoor Bootcamp

Train in the great outdoors, using a mix of the old and new training styles to perform whole body exercises. Great for fitness, strength and conditioning.

Cardiac Rehab

Specifically designed circuit sessions to aid rehabilitation following cardiac complications, surgery or treatment.

Yoga

Improve flexibility, strength and wellbeing.

BodyTone

Working all those areas with a cardio workout.

Yogalates

An excellent way to de-stress and unwind whilst improving your core strength.

Body Conditioning

Tone the whole body to include Pilates exercises.

Junior Street Dance

Learn dance moves to funky music for anyone aged 8- 15 years.


N.B. All in house class participants must be 16 years and above

External instructors – these are charged at additional costs. Please contact the instructor for more information.
