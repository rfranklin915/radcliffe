Class Descriptions

In-house classes free to members. Only £2.50 for non-members

Spin

Burn some calories & improve fitness in one go! Work at your own intensity while cycling to motivating music.

Boxercise

Energetic class using all training systems involving punches and footwork to incorporate a motivating total body workout.

TRX Class

Body weight suspension training to tone up and get fit. Suitable for all ages and abilities.

Pump FX

Progressive resistance training using weights to deliver total body workouts

Fight FX

Punch and kick your way in to knockout shape with the ultimate martial arts-inspired workout.

Outdoor Bootcamp

using nature in a circuit format to work your body in a different way

Core blast

Using bodyweight and equipment to work your whole core area, all in just 30 minutes!

Lucky Dip Circuits

depending which staff member is working the weekend, the class may be any of the above classes run by the sports centre!

Cardiac Rehab

Classes designed for those individuals who have had a Heart attack or heart disease and have completed Phase III cardiac rehab classes. They are designed to increase your activity levels following cardiac complications (£2 per session).


N.B. All in house class participants must be 16 years and above

External instructors – these are charged at additional costs. Please contact the instructor for more information.

Yogalates

Pilates & Tone

Junior Street Dance

Karate
