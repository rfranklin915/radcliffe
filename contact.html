F.A.Qs

Can I bring a guest with me to the gym?

Can I bring a guest with me to the gym?

Yes of course, its £5 for the guest and they must attend at the same time you are in the gym.

What’s the minimum contract for joining the gym?

What’s the minimum contract for joining the gym?

Just a minimum 3 months contract, 1 months written notice if you decide to leave.

Can I join the gym at 16 years of age?

Can I join the gym at 16 years of age?

Yes of course, minimum age for membership is 16 years of age.

What’s included in my gym membership?

What’s included in my gym membership?

the Gym facilities including goal setting, induction, regular on-going reviews, Casual use of the sports hall, (guests just pay £2 per hour) and our in-house fitness classes.

Do I have to be a student or member of SUS staff to join the gym?

Do I have to be a student or member of SUS staff to join the gym?

No, the facility is open to the public as we are a dual use facility between the school and community users. You can attend the gym during our opening hours of 7am-10pm Monday to Friday, 9-5pm Saturdays, Sundays and the Christmas period.
