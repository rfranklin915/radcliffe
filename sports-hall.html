Sports Hall

The sports hall can be heated or cooled to provide the right environment for a variety of sporting activities including badminton, table tennis, indoor football, netball, basketball, volleyball or cricket.

Facilities can be booked up to 7 days in advance on a casual basis for all activities. Sports hall hire is included in the gym membership (excluding Cricket nets). Want a regular weekly slot as part of a club or social event, then why not block book the sports hall for 10 week sessions. Receive a discount on the booking and peace of mind that you have your activity organised.
